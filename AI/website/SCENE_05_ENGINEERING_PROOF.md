# SCENE 05 — ENGINEERING IMPACT

## Status

**Locked**

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

Scene 05 demonstrates the real-world outcomes of GreenChronix engineering.

Rather than relying on testimonials, client logos, statistics, or marketing claims, this scene visualizes how disciplined engineering produces meaningful business impact.

The visitor should understand that every outcome originates from the same Engineering Core and Engineering Pipeline established in previous scenes.

---

# Narrative

The Engineering Pipeline reaches its final stage: **Evolve**.

Instead of ending, the energy branches outward into three interconnected Engineering Impact Domains.

The Engineering Core remains visible in the distance, reminding the visitor that every successful outcome begins with sound engineering principles.

The visitor transitions from *how* GreenChronix engineers systems to *what those systems achieve*.

---

# Emotional Goal

The visitor should feel:

* Trust
* Confidence
* Competence
* Long-term thinking
* Business value

The experience should communicate that engineering creates lasting impact rather than isolated software projects.

---

# Engineering Core State

State

Engineering Pipeline → Engineering Impact

* Every branch remains visually connected to the Core.

The Engineering Core must never disappear from view.

# Interaction

Hover

* Highlight selected impact domain.
* Increase emissive intensity.
* Animate nearby energy flow.
* Reveal concise engineering summary.

Click

* Expand the selected domain.
* Display a lightweight architecture visualization directly within the 3D world.
* Preserve visibility of the Engineering Core.
* Do not use modals or page navigation.

Keyboard

* Navigate between domains.
* Enter activates.
* Escape returns to the overview.

---

# Motion Language

* Calm
* Purposeful
* Premium
* Continuous

Energy should always communicate forward progress.

Avoid dramatic transitions or excessive animation.

# Transition to Scene 06

The Engineering Impact domains gradually dissolve.

Their energy returns toward the Engineering Core.

The Core unfolds into a layered Engineering Stack, revealing how infrastructure, data, AI, applications, and user experience work together to power intelligent systems.

The visitor should feel they are moving from outcomes back into the architecture that makes those outcomes possible.

---

# Design Principles

Engineering Impact is not presented as marketing.

It is presented as the natural consequence of disciplined engineering.

Every impact domain remains connected to the Engineering Core.

The visual language established in previous scenes must remain consistent.

---

# Acceptance Criteria

✓ Engineering Core remains visible.

✓ Pipeline branches organically into three impact domains.

✓ Hover and click interactions feel smooth.

✓ Architecture visualizations remain inside the 3D world.

✓ Typography remains readable.

✓ Transition toward Scene 06 feels natural.
