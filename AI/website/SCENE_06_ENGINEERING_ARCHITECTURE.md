# SCENE 06 — ENGINEERING ARCHITECTURE

## Status

Planned

Version: 0.1

---

# Purpose

Reveal how GreenChronix architects intelligent digital systems.

This scene explains how infrastructure, data, intelligence, applications, and user experience work together as one engineering system.

This is **not** a technology showcase.

It is a systems architecture visualization.

---

# Narrative

The three Engineering Impact domains gradually dissolve.

Their energy flows back toward the Engineering Core.

The Core unfolds into a vertical Engineering Architecture.

Each architectural layer powers the layer above it.

The visitor understands that great software is created through architecture rather than individual technologies.

---

# Layers

1. Infrastructure
2. Cloud Platform
3. Data Platform
4. Intelligence Layer
5. Business Applications
6. Experience Layer
7. Users

---

# Interaction

Hover

* Highlight active layer.
* Illuminate dependencies.
* Reveal concise explanation.

Click

* Expand the layer.
* Reveal the engineering components inside.
* Keep everything inside the 3D world.

No cards.

No modals.

No navigation.

---

# Camera

Slow vertical movement.

The camera climbs the architecture.

No spinning.

No sudden transitions.

---
