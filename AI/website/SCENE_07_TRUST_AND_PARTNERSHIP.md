# SCENE 07 — TRUST & PARTNERSHIP

## Status

Planned

Version: 0.1

---

# Purpose

Demonstrate how GreenChronix works with clients throughout an engineering engagement.

This scene focuses on collaboration, transparency, and long-term partnership rather than sales.

---

# Narrative

The Engineering Architecture transforms into a collaborative engineering ecosystem.

Energy flows from GreenChronix to a client system and back again, illustrating continuous feedback and long-term evolution.

---

# Visual Concept

Two connected systems.

Left:
GreenChronix Engineering Core.

Right:
Client Digital Ecosystem.

Between them:

- Secure data channels
- Synchronization pulses
- Bidirectional energy flow
- Continuous monitoring

The relationship should resemble an engineering partnership, not a transaction.

---

# Camera

Slow orbit around both systems.

Keep both ecosystems visible.

No sudden movements.

---

# Interaction

Hover

- Highlight connection paths.
- Reveal collaboration stages.

Click

- Expand the selected collaboration point.
- Show engineering responsibilities.
- Keep all interactions inside the 3D world.
