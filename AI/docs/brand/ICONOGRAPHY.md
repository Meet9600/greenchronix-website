Iconography

Purpose

Icons support comprehension.

They should never replace clear labels.

⸻

Style

Minimal.

Modern.

Consistent.

Geometric.

Rounded corners where appropriate.

Simple strokes.

⸻

Rules

Maintain a single icon family across the product.

Avoid mixing styles.

Icons should reinforce meaning rather than decorate.

Keep sizing consistent.

⸻

Usage

Navigation.

Feature highlights.

Status indicators.

Interactive controls.

Documentation.

Never overload pages with icons.

⸻

Accessibility

Icons must include accessible labels when required.

Do not rely on icons alone to communicate critical information.

⸻

Closing Statement

Icons should reduce cognitive effort.

Every icon should make the interface easier to understand.

End of Document.
