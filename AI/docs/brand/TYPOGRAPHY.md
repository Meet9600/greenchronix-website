GreenChronix Typography

Purpose

Typography defines how GreenChronix communicates visually.

It should reflect precision, confidence, and readability.

⸻

Principles

Typography creates hierarchy before decoration.

Readable text is always more important than expressive text.

Consistency is more valuable than variety.

⸻

Font Strategy

Primary Typeface

A modern geometric sans-serif for all interfaces.

Examples:

* Geist
* Inter
* IBM Plex Sans

Headings should feel confident and spacious.

Body text should remain highly readable across devices.

⸻

Hierarchy

Display

Large hero statements.

H1

Primary page titles.

H2

Section titles.

H3

Subsections.

Body

Primary content.

Caption

Supporting information.

⸻

Rules

Avoid excessive font weights.

Avoid decorative fonts.

Use generous line height.

Maintain strong visual rhythm.

Limit font families.

⸻

Accessibility

Maintain readable contrast.

Support responsive scaling.

Never reduce readability for visual style.

⸻

Closing Statement

Typography is the voice users see before they read.

Every typographic decision should improve understanding.
